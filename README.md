## ğŸ“‰ Key Insights


1. The overall customer **churn rate stands at 16.73%**, indicating the proportion of customers lost over the period.
2. Churn varies by city tier, with **Tier 1 cities at 57% and Tier 3 cities at 37%**, showing higher customer attrition in major urban areas.
3. In the category of **satisfaction score 3, 265 customers churned, primarily due to 441 total complaints**.
4. Customer satisfaction decreases as the distance from the warehouse to their home increases.
5. The company offers lower cashback to customers whose homes are farther from the warehousem, which is unfavorable for the business.
